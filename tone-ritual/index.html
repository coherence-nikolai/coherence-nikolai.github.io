TONE RITUAL

Ten small acts. One changed field.

Choose a ritual, receive ten small acts, complete any that feel possible, and save a quiet field note for the day you are actually in.

What it does

A ritual app, not a productivity tracker.

Tone Ritual offers small wellbeing acts that support steadiness, gratitude, kindness, connection, courage, repair, presence, and emotional coherence. You do not have to complete everything. You choose what is possible.

Inside the app

Rituals you can actually do.

Choose the ritual that matches the day, move through ten small acts, and keep the note that matters.

Ritual flow

Four simple movements.

01

Choose

Pick the ritual that fits your state, energy, or threshold.

02

Receive

Get ten small acts drawn from a much larger ritual field.

03

Complete

Do any acts that feel possible. Leave the rest without shame.

04

Save

Keep a quiet field note so something from the ritual can return.
