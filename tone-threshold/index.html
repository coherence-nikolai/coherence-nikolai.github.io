TONE THRESHOLD

A contemplative doorway.

Stand before a question long enough for the answer to fall silent. Tone Threshold is a private, quiet crossing for inquiry, return, and the thresholds that do not become tasks.

Coming soon on the App Store Support

What it does

A question as a doorway.

Tone Threshold is not a journal, meditation timer, quote app, therapy tool, or productivity system. It offers contemplative questions that act like thresholds: places to stand, not problems to solve.

Inside the app

One question. One doorway.

Begin with a single free crossing, then open deeper rooms through Tone Plus: Return Field, guided crossings, premium doors, and the Personal Chamber.

Crossing flow

Three quiet movements.

01

Stand

Receive a question without rushing to answer it.

02

Cross

Let the doorway open, the old question fade, and the next one arrive.

03

Keep

Save a mark when something in the question has not finished with you.

04

Return

Tone Plus can bring saved questions back through Return Field.

Boundaries

A contemplative inquiry app.

Tone Threshold is for reflection and contemplative inquiry. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, crisis support, or an emergency service.
