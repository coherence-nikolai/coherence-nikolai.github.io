Five eight-spoked dhamma wheel options

Each route keeps eight spokes for the Noble Eightfold Path and stays inside the premium dark and gold Unfabricate palette.

Option A

Classic Wheel

Canonical symbol

The most immediately recognisable dhamma wheel: clear rim, hub, and eight path spokes.

Best for: Primary landing hero and formal brand lockup.

Watch: It is the most traditional option, so the surrounding typography needs to carry the app's own voice.

8 spokes verified

Option B

Seal Tile

App icon

A compact rounded-square seal that keeps the wheel readable at tiny sizes.

Best for: Favicon, home-screen icon, sidebar, and small navigation marks.

Watch: More product-like than ceremonial; less airy in large hero placements.

8 spokes verified

Selected option

Lotus Hub

Soft ceremonial

Keeps the eight spokes while softening the center with a quiet lotus-like rhythm.

Best for: A warmer identity where practice, ease, and release should feel more present.

Watch: The extra detail needs size testing before using it as the smallest app icon.

8 spokes verified

Option D

Path Compass

Minimal system mark

Reduces the wheel to a precise pathfinder mark with eight clear directions.

Best for: A clean digital product identity with strong small-size performance.

Watch: It reads more contemporary and less devotional than the classic wheel.

8 spokes verified

Option E

Coin Wheel

Premium medallion

A richer medallion with eight rim points, built to feel weighty and finished.

Best for: Premium headers, loading states, and high-polish brand moments.

Watch: The darker filled center may be too heavy if the rest of the UI becomes dense.

8 spokes verified
