A quiet guide for serious practice.

Structure without noise.

What it is

A practice companion.

Use it to time sits, track practice, orient to insight maps, and return to direct experience.

Who it is for

Serious practitioners.

For people who want meditation support that is structured, restrained, and not socially noisy.

What it helps with

Orientation and continuity.

Keep track of sits, noting, reflections, stages, and the next honest practice step.

Privacy note

Practice is not content.

No ads. No social feed. Built as a private instrument for practice, not extraction.
