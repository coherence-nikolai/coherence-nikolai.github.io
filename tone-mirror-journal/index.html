What it does

A resonance chamber for symbolic reflection.

Tone Mirror pairs signal, glyph, breath, and tone so reflection can feel shaped rather than merely described. The chamber is local-first, private by design, and built to let patterns return gently through replay and archive memory.

The chamber

Three calm steps.

01

Shape

Choose the signal that best matches the moment. The chamber suggests glyph and breath around it.

02

Breathe

Settle the field through tone, cadence, and a focal glyph that holds the signal in view.

03

Write

Reveal layered reflection, add a phase note if you want, and save the moment as harmonic memory.

Replay

Return

Re-enter a saved memory through sound, breath, glyph, and mirrored language instead of treating it like a note.

Core

Signal first.

Begin with the core seven signals, open the wider field only if needed, and let glyph and breath remain refinements rather than setup burdens.

Memory

Patterns can return.

Archive memories can echo, return, shift, or complete. The mirror becomes more observant over time without turning into a social feed or a generic AI assistant.

Privacy

Local-first and held on-device.

Tone Mirror is built as a private reflection instrument. The core mirror is non-AI, offline-capable, and does not require an account to begin.

Boundaries

A symbolic reflection tool.

Tone Mirror is for reflection, pattern language, attention, and self-guided return. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, crisis support, or an emergency service.
