What it does

Practice the state you choose.

The app helps you choose a mind state, tune it until it feels true, hold it, and recall it later. Premium resonance chambers let you enter different rooms around the same tone without replacing it. Spiral Practice guides a longer return through ground, listen, name, and next step.

Inside the app

Choose, tune, hold, and return.

The interface stays quiet so the sound can lead. No account, no feed, no pressure to perform.

Why it matters

Repetition makes a state easier to return to.

Tone Recall is not about tracking or optimizing yourself. It is a small, private practice for becoming familiar with a chosen state through sound, feeling, and repetition.

Boundaries

A personal reflection tool.

Tone Recall is made for choosing, holding, and returning to a mind state through sound. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, crisis support, or an emergency service.
