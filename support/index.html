Support

Need help with Tone Recall?

Email: support@coherence-nikolai.app

When you write

Please include your device model, iOS version, Tone Recall app version, what you were doing when the issue happened, and whether it involved free tones, premium chambers, recall, held tones, sharing, or restore purchases.

Common topics

  • Restore purchases: open Tone Recall, go to Settings, and tap restore purchases.
  • Audio issue: include the exact path, for example "Love to Vessel to hold."
  • Privacy question: email with the subject "Tone Recall privacy question."
  • Bug report: include steps to reproduce and screenshots or screen recordings if helpful.

Response time

Expected response time: 3 to 5 business days.
