When you write

Please include your device model, iOS version or browser, app name, app version if available, what you were doing when the issue happened, and whether it involved guidance, audio, premium access, recall, rituals, Tone Mirror memories, sharing, local data, or restore purchases.

Common topics

  • Restore purchases: open the relevant Tone app, go to Settings, and tap restore purchases.
  • Sota issue: include whether it involved sitting, noting, the stage map, practice logs, provider keys, AI guidance, or the web version.
  • Audio issue: include the exact app and path, for example "Tone Recall, Love to Vessel to hold."
  • Tone Threshold issue: include the selected door, crossing mode, and whether the issue involved Return Field, Personal Chamber, audio, or Tone Plus.
  • Tone Mirror issue: include the browser, selected symbol, glyph, and whether the issue involved breath, sound, or archive replay.
  • Privacy question: email with the subject "Tone app privacy question."
  • Bug report: include steps to reproduce and screenshots or screen recordings if helpful.

Response time

Expected response time: 3 to 5 business days.
