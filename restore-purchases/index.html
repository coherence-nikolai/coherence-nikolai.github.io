Restore Purchases

Tone Recall premium is currently a one-time Apple In-App Purchase. There is no recurring subscription in the current iOS app.

How to restore

  1. Open Tone Recall.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap restore purchases.
  4. Wait for the restore to complete.

If restore does not work

  • Confirm you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Try restore purchases again.

Refunds

Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple, not directly by Tone Recall.

For refunds, use reportaproblem.apple.com.
