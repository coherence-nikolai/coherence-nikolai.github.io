Tone: Hidden Worlds

Boundaries

Curiosity must not become certainty.

The app offers possible worlds to consider. It should never be used to label someone, excuse harm, avoid a needed conversation, or override your own boundaries.

If a situation involves immediate danger, abuse, self-harm, coercion, medical concerns, legal risk, or emergency needs, use qualified local support instead of the app.

The core practice is simple: look deeper, assume less, and stay human.
