    experiment stance

    No doctrine. Direct observation.

    I-Sense Observatory does not tell you what the self is. It helps you observe the felt sense of "me" and record what happens.

    A session may feel ordinary, unclear, subtle, intense, spacious, or uneventful. Each of those is a valid field note.

    No belief required The app does not ask you to adopt a view.
    No promised result Dropping away, wide perception, and no change are all recorded neutrally.
    Your observation The app points. The result is what you directly notice.

    If this feels too intense, stop the experiment. Look around the room. Feel the body in contact with the surface beneath it. Return when you want to.
