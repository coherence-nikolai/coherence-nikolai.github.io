Tone suite

Three apps. One underlying practice.

You are always giving off a tone. These apps help you listen to it, shape it, steady it, and carry it with more coherence.

Coherence Lab

Quiet software for sensitive attention.

These tools began as experiments in attention, breath, pattern, learning, and regulation. The aim is not to make louder products. The aim is to make small instruments that help a person steady, notice, and continue.
