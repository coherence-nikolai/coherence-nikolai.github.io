Founder note

About Coherence Nikolai.

I’m Nikolai, founder of Coherence Nikolai and creator of the Tone app family.

All of my creations are aimed at coherence and tone: the felt sense of things becoming more honest, more integrated, and more alive, I’m interested in contemplation, self-inquiry, consciousness, and the small shifts of attention that change how we meet ourselves and the world.

I create quiet private tools for noticing mind states, returning to clarity, and living with more presence. All in order to support the raising of the frequency for these evolving times.

As an academic skills advisor, I also support people to think, write, learn, and move through complexity with more confidence and coherence.
