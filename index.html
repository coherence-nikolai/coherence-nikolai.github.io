Northstar

Study support that lowers the cost of starting again.

Designed for neurodivergent adult students who need clarity, re-entry, and a calmer first step without being talked down to.

Northstar does not ask a student to feel ready first. It helps them begin, understand a task, capture what matters, and stop in a way they can actually return to later.

Coherence Lab

Quiet software for sensitive attention and adult dignity.

These tools began as experiments in attention, breath, pattern, learning, and regulation. The aim is not to make louder products. The aim is to make small, well-made instruments that help a person orient, steady, and continue.
