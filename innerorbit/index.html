Inner Orbit

A symbolic paradox practice inspired by mathematical, geometric, and consciousness-related themes explored in the works of Robert Edward Grant. Independently created.

The copper loop holds what is present. The blue field holds what is also true. The pale-gold center is where attention practices holding both.

Breath mode is inspired by Grant's breath-as-lens framing, translated here into a simple visual guide.

  • Inhale: follow the blue hyperboloid orb.
  • Pause: let the pale-gold focal ring shine.
  • Exhale: follow the copper toroidal loop.
  • Rest: let the whole field settle.
