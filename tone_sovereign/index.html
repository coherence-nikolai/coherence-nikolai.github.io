TONE SOVEREIGN

Choose what governs you.

Tone Sovereign is a private five-step instrument for noticing what has authority, revoking it, choosing a new tone, holding it, and saving the shift locally.

What it does

Strip suffering of its authority.

Name the pressure, fear, story, or demand that has been steering your attention. Then choose the tone you want to govern the next moment.

The web app plays a simple holding tone, guides a short breath-like hold, and saves sessions only on your device unless you export them.

Tone Sovereign is for self-reflection and state practice. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, crisis support, or an emergency service.
