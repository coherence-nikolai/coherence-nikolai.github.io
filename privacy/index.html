Tone Recall

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 April 2026

Overview

Tone Recall does not require an account or sign-in.

The app is designed as a local-first audio and reflection instrument. Held tones, chamber memories, return history, and local shared-chamber records are stored on your device so you can return to them later.

Local information

Tone Recall stores the tones and chamber memories you choose to keep, plus app settings needed for the experience to work.

Sharing and exports

Tone Recall does not automatically send your held tones to other people or services. If you export or share something, the app uses Apple's share sheet and you choose the destination.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple. Tone Recall receives the purchase status needed to unlock or restore premium chamber access, but does not collect your full payment card details.

Tracking and advertising

The current iOS app does not use advertising SDKs or account-based tracking.

Remote sharing

The v1 iOS app does not use networked tone sharing. If remote sharing is added later, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before launch.

Deleting data

To remove local Tone Recall data, delete saved items in the app where available or delete the app from your device.
